Otaku Calendar

Welcome to the Otaku Calendar! This tool allows you to generate a personalized anime calendar for the week based on the latest anime schedules. The calendar can be exported as an .ics file that you can add to your personal calendar app to track airing dates and times.

Follow these steps to generate your anime calendar:

  • 1. Click the Generate Anime Calendar button below.
  • 2. The tool will automatically fetch anime schedules for the entire week from Monday to Sunday.
  • 3. Once the data is gathered, it will generate an .ics file containing the anime schedules, which will be downloaded to your device.
  • 4. Add the .ics file to your preferred calendar application (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.) to stay updated with anime airing times!
  • 5. Enjoy your personalized anime schedule.
